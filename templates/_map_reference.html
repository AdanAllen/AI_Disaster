How to read this map

Address point checkedThe selected coordinate was compared with an available official layer.

Mapped match foundThe point falls inside that dataset's boundary. This does not predict damage.

No mapped matchThe point is outside the displayed boundary; related hazards may still occur.

Official source, provisional StayReady integrationUseful for planning, not a legal, engineering, insurance, or safety determination.

Layer definitions and sources

FEMA flood zones: Floodplain-management and insurance mapping; not every flood mechanism.

CAL FIRE zones: Long-term wildfire hazard conditions; not live fire or evacuation tracking.

CGS liquefaction: Areas where strong shaking may cause wet, loose soil to lose strength.

CGS landslide: Slopes where earthquake shaking may trigger landslides.

Fault zones: Surface-fault context; not a forecast of shaking or damage.

Tsunami areas: Coastal hazard and evacuation-planning areas.
