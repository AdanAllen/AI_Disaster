Help with map and evidence terms
FEMA Zone XA FEMA mapped category generally outside the Special Flood Hazard Area or with lower or undetermined mapped flood hazard. It does not mean flooding cannot occur.
Liquefaction zoneSome soils may lose strength during strong shaking. This does not predict damage to a specific building.
Alquist-Priolo Fault ZoneA California regulatory zone around active fault traces used for surface-fault-rupture review.
Earthquake-induced landslide zoneAn area where strong shaking may trigger landslides. It is not a property-level slope inspection.
Tsunami hazard areaAn official planning area for possible tsunami flooding or evacuation. Follow local instructions during an event.
