Quick Emergency Tips

First 72 Hours

Emergency responders may not be able to reach you immediately. Have supplies to last 72 hours minimum.

Know Your Routes

Identify multiple evacuation routes from your home, work, and children's schools.

Important Documents

Keep copies of insurance, medical records, and ID in a waterproof container.

Communication Plan

Choose an out-of-state contact person and make sure everyone knows their number.

Power Backup

Keep phone chargers, portable batteries, and a battery-powered or hand-crank radio.

Home Safety

Know how to turn off utilities (gas, water, electricity) and keep tools accessible.
